Kehan Long

I am a PhD student in Mathematics and Contextual Robotics Institute at University of California, San-Diego. I work at the Existential Robotics Laboratory and the Computational Geometric Mechanics Group. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Nikolay Atanasov and Prof. Melvin Leok. I also worked closely with Prof. Jorge Cortes. I received my B.S. degree in applied mathematics with a minor in computer engineering from the University of Illinois,Urbana-Champaign.
